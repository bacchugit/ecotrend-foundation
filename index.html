About Us

Eco Trend Foundation is a non-governmental organization focused on protecting and conserving natural ecosystems through research, capacity building, advocacy, and alternative livelihood training for local communities. The organization is located in the Bia West District, Western North Region of Ghana.

Our work is grounded in the One Health approach, which recognizes the interdependence between human well-being, ecosystem integrity and sustainable livelihoods. By addressing environmental conservation, community health and economic resilience together, Eco Trend Foundation delivers integrated solutions that are locally relevant and globally aligned.

Our Mission

To empower communities through sustainable environmental protection, education, and livelihood initiatives that promote social equity, climate resilience, and long-term development.

Our Vision

A future where communities live in harmony with nature, thrive economically, and actively lead efforts toward environmental sustainability and inclusive development.

Our One Health Approach

Eco Trend Foundation adopts a One Health approach that recognizes the interconnection between healthy ecosystems, resilient communities, and sustainable livelihoods. We understand that protecting the environment reduces disease risks, strengthens food systems and improves overall quality of life.

Through integrated conservation, education and livelihood interventions, we address environmental degradation, climate risks and socio-economic vulnerabilities in a coordinated manner that delivers measurable and long-term impact.

This integrated One Health framework guides all our programs and partnerships, shaping how we design, implement and scale solutions for environmental conservation, community Empowerment and sustainable development.

Our Work

At Eco Trend Foundation, we combine scientific expertise, community engagement, and innovative approaches to deliver sustainable solutions. Our work spans environmental conservation, education and capacity building, and economic empowerment, creating lasting impact for communities, ecosystems, and future generations. These interventions directly support One Health outcomes by maintaining healthy ecosystems that underpin human and animal well-being.

Environmental Conservation

We implement evidence-based strategies for forest protection, biodiversity conservation, and ecosystem restoration. Our interventions safeguard critical habitats, enhance ecological resilience, and promote long-term sustainability for local communities and wildlife.

Education & Capacity Building

Through workshops, training sessions, and community engagement, we empower individuals and groups with the knowledge, skills, and awareness necessary to drive sustainable environmental and social change.

Our education programs integrate One Health principles, strengthening community understanding of the links between environmental protection, public health and sustainable livelihoods.

Livelihoods & Economic Empowerment

We support alternative livelihood initiatives and income-generating activities, providing communities with sustainable economic opportunities while promoting responsible natural resource management and long-term resilience.

These initiatives align with the One Health framework by reducing environmental pressure, improving household resilience and supporting healthier interactions between people and their natural Environment.
